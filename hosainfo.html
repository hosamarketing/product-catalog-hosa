The Weekly Action Plan

Monday: Strategy & Performance Review

Kick off the week by analyzing past performance and planning future actions.

  • Review last week's campaign data (Display, Search, Programmatic).
  • Analyze lead generation KPIs (MQLs, SQLs, CPA).
  • Brainstorm and plan this week's content calendar.
  • Sync with sales and marketing teams on goals.

Tuesday: Campaign & Content Execution

Focus on optimizing live campaigns and creating new assets.

  • Launch A/B tests for ad creatives and landing pages.
  • Adjust campaign budgets and audience targeting.
  • Draft blog posts and script videos.

Wednesday: Publishing & SEO

Push content live and optimize the digital storefront.

  • Publish new blog posts and videos.
  • Distribute content via social media and email newsletters.
  • Conduct keyword research and on-page SEO audits.

Thursday: Community & Direct Outreach

Engage directly with the community and high-value prospects.

  • Participate in industry forums and LinkedIn groups.
  • Identify and research high-potential leads.
  • Craft and send personalized outreach emails.

Friday: Analysis & Planning

Report on the week's results and prepare for the next cycle.

  • Compile and analyze the weekly performance report.
  • Review A/B test results and implement winners.
  • Refine strategy and outline tasks for the upcoming week.
